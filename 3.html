ANTIKNIGT SUDOKU

Anti Knight Sudoku puzzle is Classic Sudoku look alike puzzle. In this Sudoku Classic Sudoku rules are applied along with one added chess rule that no two identical number can be chess knight's move away from each other. Anti Knight Sudoku is also known with the name No Knight Step Sudoku as same numbers on the cells which are Knight Step away from each other are not allowed.
